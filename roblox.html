ROBLOX

UI/UX Project: Parental Control System Redesign

Designed by: Huaqian Zheng, Aakanskha Soni, Hein Tun

Project Brief

Parents are concerned about their children's excessive spending and screen time on Roblox. This affects family finances, academic performance, and can expose them to online risks. Our goal is to design a solution that empowers parents to manage their children's gaming habits effectively.

Goal

To resolve the concern that parents of children (13 and under) have about excessive time and money spent on Roblox.

Empathy Map

Understanding the parent's perspective by mapping what they say, think, do, and feel about their children's Roblox usage.

Says

"I'm worried about how much money my kids are spending on this game. Their grades are slipping too."

Thinks & Feels

Anxious about online safety, guilty for not being able to monitor them effectively, and frustrated with the lack of control.

Does

Checks bank statements frequently, tries to set verbal limits, and seeks advice from other parents online.

Sees

Kids glued to screens for hours, notifications for in-game purchases, and signs of eye strain in her children.

Persona

Sarah Williamson

40, School Teacher

"I need a simple way to protect my kids and our wallet without being a helicopter parent."

Bio

Sarah is a tech-savvy mother of two (11 & 14) who loves her kids but is increasingly worried about their digital habits on Roblox. She wants tools that help her guide them responsibly.

Core Needs

  • Peace of mind about finances
  • Ensure online safety
  • Balance gaming with school

Frustrations

  • Unexpected in-game charges
  • Complex existing control apps
  • Arguments over screen time

Sarah's Journey

A storyboard visualizing a parent's path from concern to control with the new parental control system.

User Flow

Mapping the parent's journey from downloading the companion app to successfully setting up spending and time limits for their child's account.

Project Solution

A Smart Companion App for Parents

A dedicated Roblox companion app that provides parents with a simple dashboard to monitor spending, track playing time, and set intelligent limits. Proactive notifications alert parents before issues arise, fostering healthier gaming habits.
