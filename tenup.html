TENUP

Mathematics can be a challenging subject for young students, often leading to frustration and disengagement.

The TENUP project aims to transform this experience by creating an interactive and enjoyable mobile application tailored for 2nd-grade students. Our goal is to foster a love for math through engaging activities that reinforce core concepts like place value, addition, and subtraction, ensuring that learning is both effective and fun.

The Problem

Place Value

Struggling to grasp tens and ones, and difficulty comparing and ordering numbers.

Addition & Subtraction

Finding it hard to carry over or borrow, and misplacing digits in problems.

Word Problems

Struggling to read and comprehend problems or translate words into operations.

Overview

★ Background

Tenup focuses on Grade Two Mathematics education. It aligns with Grade Two Mathematics standards and aims to encourage students to:

(1) develop conceptual understanding

(2) use drawings instead of writing equations

(3) solve word problems to aid comprehension and explanation.

The target audience is Grade 2 students, ages 7 to 8.

★ Goal

To help users understand place value & develop conceptual understanding of core math principles.

★ Persona 2

"“ I'm worried about my son Sam's math performance, and I want to help him!”"

Austin, TX, USA

35 years old

ABOUT

  • Mary is Sam's mom, who is worried about how to explain the concepts of 2nd-grade mathematics to help him improve his grades.
  • Sam's teacher has reminded her to assist with his mathematics out of the class.

NEED

  • • Help her to explain the 2nd-grade math concepts to her son
