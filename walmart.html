Research

Business Goal

  • Expand branding influence between young users (age 18-30).
  • Generate user stickiness among young users to motivate continuous consumption.
  • Attract more young users (age 18-30) by focusing on AR/AI/VR integration.

Company Background

  • Everyday Low Prices: Aimed at budget-conscious shoppers, including families and individuals.
  • One-Stop Shop: Customers can find a wide variety of products under one roof.
  • Senior Citizens: Appreciate affordable prices on fixed incomes.
  • Rural Communities: Serves as a primary shopping destination in many rural areas.

User Analysis

Targeted Users Costs & Popular Apps

The target users (age 18-30) tend to spend a significant amount on social activities. Social media applications are the most popular apps among this demographic.

Popular AR/VR Functions

The target users are more interested in VR/AR features related to social interactions.

Gaming: Immersive VR gaming experiences (Oculus Rift, PS VR).
Social VR: Platforms for social interaction in VR (VRChat, AltspaceVR).
AR Filters on Social Media: Effects on Instagram and Snapchat.
Virtual Tours: Explore destinations, museums, and landmarks.

User Needs

More community connections Save more money More convenience

Interview with Daniel

Daniel

Bookkeeper, 26 years old

Q: What motivates you to shop online?

A: Online shopping makes my life easier... especially when it turns out that my refrigerator is empty and I urgently need to buy groceries. Sometimes I really need fast delivery.

Q: Have you ever used virtual try-on features while shopping?

A: Yes, I have. I found out that Adidas had launched an augmented reality feature... It was a very cool experience for me and I think it would be great if more brands used this feature.

Q: What do you usually buy at Walmart?

A: When I was a student, I would often visit Walmart to find school and dorm supplies at the best price... Now, I prefer to buy groceries online from Walmart.

Q: What do you think of the Walmart app?

A: I think the app is fine, but sometimes it glitches and that really bothers me. In general, it is easy to select and order items. I also like that the app shows me exclusive online offers.

Q: Could you please tell me more about your shopping experience at Walmart?

A: I don't like to visit Walmart because I don't feel comfortable... I think Walmart is mostly visited by older people rather than young adults like me.

Q: What would attract you to buy necessary things in Walmart?

A: I think good deals and offers can increase my interest in shopping at Walmart because I don't want to spend too much money on essentials.
